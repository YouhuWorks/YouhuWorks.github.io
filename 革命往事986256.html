《革命往事》是一款以中國近現代史為核心的AOH 2 MOD，時間線橫跨辛亥革命的炮火、軍閥割據的動盪、全民族抗戰的烽火，直至解放戰爭的勝利與新中國的誕生。

模組遵循歷史史實，融入海量紅色元素與愛國題材內容，透過沉浸式的遊戲玩法，讓你親歷那段救亡圖存、砥礪前行的崢嶸歲月。

Revolutionary Memories is an AOH 2 MOD focused on the modern and contemporary history of China, spanning from the 1911 Revolution, the warlord separatism, the full-scale War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, to the victory of the Liberation War and the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The MOD abides by historical facts, integrates a large number of red elements and patriotic themes, and allows you to experience the extraordinary years of national salvation and forging ahead through immersive gameplay.

核心历史阶段 核心歷史階段 Core Historical Stages

  • 1911-1926共和初建与军阀割据：从武昌起义的枪响到北伐战争的号角，见证共和理想在乱世中的坚守。
  • 1927-1937土地革命与民族危亡：南昌城头的枪声、井冈山的星火、长征的壮举，在民族危机中点燃救亡希望。
  • 1937-1945全民族抗战：卢沟桥事变揭开抗战序幕，国共携手、军民同心，浴血奋战迎来最终胜利。
  • 1945-1949解放全中国：从双十协定到三大战役，从渡江战役到新中国成立，终结独裁统治，开启民族新纪元。
  • 1911-1926共和初建與軍閥割據：從武昌起義的槍響到北伐戰爭的號角，見證共和理想在亂世中的堅守。
  • 1927-1937土地革命與民族危亡：南昌城頭的槍聲、井岡山的星火、長征的壯舉，在民族危機中點燃救亡希望。
  • 1937-1945全民族抗戰：盧溝橋事變揭開抗戰序幕，國共攜手、軍民同心，浴血奮戰迎來最終勝利。
  • 1945-1949解放全中國：從雙十協定到三大戰役，從渡江戰役到新中國成立，終結獨裁統治，開啟民族新紀元。
  • 1911-1926Founding of the Republic & Warlord Separatism: From the Wuchang Uprising to the Northern Expedition, witness the perseverance of republican ideals in troubled times.
  • 1927-1937Land Revolution & National Crisis: The gunshots in Nanchang, the spark in Jinggangshan, the feat of the Long March, ignite the hope of national salvation in the national crisis.
  • 1937-1945Full-scale National Resistance War: The Lugou Bridge Incident opened the prelude to the war, the KMT and CCP joined hands, the military and civilians were of one heart, and fought bloody battles for the final victory.
  • 1945-1949Liberation of China: From the Double Tenth Agreement to the Three Major Campaigns, from the Crossing the Yangtze Campaign to the founding of New China, end the autocratic rule and open a new era for the nation.
